Hack Kean 2022

Kean University's Annual Hackathon

Join us for a day online as we build apps, projects, and more. We will provide a comfortable and enlivening atmosphere for you to build out progressive, innovative, and crazy ideas. At HackKean, you'll meet fellow hackers, learn new technologies, and hone your skills. We'll have prizes, workshops, and more... Don't have a team or an idea? Don't worry! We'll connect you with others and give you the tools to build something incredible. Whether you're a seasoned hackathon-goer or just getting started, we'd love to see you here!
