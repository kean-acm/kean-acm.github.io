Hack Kean 2022

Kean University's Annual Hackathon

Join us for a day online as we build apps, projects, and more. We will provide a comfortable and enlivening atmosphere for you to build out progressive, innovative, and crazy ideas. At HackKean, you'll meet fellow hackers, learn new technologies, and hone your skills. We'll have prizes, workshops, and more... Don't have a team or an idea? Don't worry! We'll connect you with others and give you the tools to build something incredible. Whether you're a seasoned hackathon-goer or just getting started, we'd love to see you here!

Guidlines

Regestration Guidlines

  1. Participants must be students currently pursuing k-12 education, a graduate degree, or an undergraduate degree.
  2. Participants may register as a team of no more than 3 individuals. Prizes are to be shared among the individuals.
  3. Registration must be complete before the registration deadline on February 12th, unless stated otherwise by the Kean ACM team.

Participation Guidlines

  1. Work must be done by the participants. If any outside sources are used (excluding public libraries or APIs), they are to be documented. You may use code snippets found online if the source is documented. Using code without including its source may result in your disqualification. Copying an entire project or most of a project may also result in disqualification.
  2. Be respectful to other participants. Bullying and harassment of other participants will not be tolerated and will result in immediate disqualification
  3. Recording of the event or any workshop is not allowed. Recordings may be provided later by the ACM, but participants are not to record the event or any workshop within the event.
  4. Do not share internal links and information without permission.
  5. Violating any of the above guidelines may result in disqualification. The decision to disqualify participants is ultimately up to Kean University and the Kean ACM team.
