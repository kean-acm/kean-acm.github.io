About Hack Kean 2023

Join us for a day of coding fun as you compete against others to build whatever coding related project you would like to make. We will provide a comfortable and enlivening atmosphere for you to build out progressive, innovative, or fun crazy ideas. At Hack Kean, you'll meet fellow programmers, learn new technologies, and hone your skills. We'll have prizes, workshops, food, and more... Whether you're a seasoned hackathon-goer or are brand new, we'd love to see you here!
